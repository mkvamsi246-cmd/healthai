FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of medical reports can I upload?
HealthInsight AI supports blood test reports, complete blood counts (CBC), lipid profiles, diabetes panels (HbA1c, glucose), kidney function tests, thyroid panels, liver function tests, and more. Accepted formats include PDF, JPG, and PNG.
Is my health data safe and private?
Absolutely. Your health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption, stored in HIPAA-compliant secure servers, and never shared with any third party. You maintain full control and can delete your data at any time.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
Our AI achieves 98%+ accuracy in parameter extraction and interpretation. It's trained on millions of anonymized medical records and validated by certified healthcare professionals. Always consult a doctor for final medical decisions.
Can I compare reports from different labs?
Yes! HealthInsight AI normalizes data from any lab format. You can compare reports from different labs, different time periods, or different diagnostic centers — all in one unified view.
Is HealthInsight AI free to use?
HealthInsight AI offers a free tier with basic analysis for up to 3 reports per month. Our Pro plan (₹799/month) offers unlimited reports, advanced risk predictions, detailed progress tracking, and priority support.
